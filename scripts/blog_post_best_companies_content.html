How to Choose the Right Real Estate Company

When selecting a real estate partner in Abuja, consider:

  • Verification: Ensure properties have C of O or R of O from FCDA/AGIS.
  • Track Record: Look for completed projects you can inspect.
  • Transparency: Clear pricing, no hidden fees.
  • Payment Flexibility: Installment options for easier access.
  • Customer Reviews: Check Google reviews and testimonials.

Before making any purchase, always protect yourself from land scams by conducting proper due diligence. Whether you're buying in Maitama, Asokoro, or Gwarinpa, working with a verified company ensures a smooth transaction.
